About Me

Hi, I am Juliana and I am currently an Interactive Media Design student at Algonquin College in Ottawa, CA. I am 23 and I have a degree in Design from Los Andes University in Colombia from which I graduated in 2021. I have a passion for art, design and visual graphics but I have recently discovered a huge interest in web development. Growing up, I always learned through visual aids and today I am glad to be able to communicate with others through graphic design.

This is my 3rd a freelance designer; creating both print and digital designs for different companies with opposing visual styles. I can say I am very versatile when it comes to adapting to contrasting branding styles, which is an advantage in today's fast paced industry. I also have 2 years of experience as a graphic designer in an advertising company. This is where I learned teamwork and efficiency to be able to deliver several creative designs every day.
